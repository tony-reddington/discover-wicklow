Wicklow lies on the Eastern coast of Ireland just 40 minutes South of Dublin. It’s colloquially known as the "Garden of Ireland" and has been popular for many years due to it’s incredible scenery, beaches, walking and hiking options. It’s a place of endless natural beauty and captivating history that, with the help of friendly locals, has returning tourists year after year...
